The Method

A cycle, not a curriculum.

The Prism Method™ is a process framework. You return to each phase differently every time — because you are different every time.

01 — Observe

Notice what is present.

In the body, in language, in patterns of communication. Without judgment — just clear seeing.

02 — Reflect

Make meaning together.

Through honest dialogue and structured inquiry. Understanding before strategy.

03 — Practice

Experiment in real conditions.

Role-play, scenario work, movement, creative expression. Learning that lives in the body, not just the mind.

04 — Integrate

Carry it forward.

Into exams, workplaces, daily life and relationship. Sustainable change, not performance.

Philosophy

"Real learning doesn't happen to us. It happens through us — when the body, the voice, and the mind are all in the room."

How This Works

  • Sessions are structured around your actual goal — exam, credential, transition, or personal growth
  • Communication is treated as a whole-body skill, not just vocabulary or grammar
  • Dialogue drives the process — your experience is the primary material
  • Emotional intelligence and nervous system awareness are embedded throughout
  • Progress is measured in real capability, not completion of modules
  • Collaboration with aligned practitioners is part of the model

Kiprisma is a fluid navigation through diverse mediums.

The name begins with Ki — life force, the animating energy that moves through and connects. Not a metaphor. The organizing principle of everything that follows.

Christopher Michael has worked at the intersection of language, body, learning, and human systems for over thirty years — across Brazil and the United States. Trained in psychology and management, grounded in movement, music, and the martial art of Ki Aikido, his practice draws on a full spectrum of human intelligence and communicative form.

Currently based in the greater Boston area. Working between Boston and New York City, and online.

The practice began in Brazil in the early 1990s — in dance conservatory, contact improvisation, physical theater, and the language institutions of Curitiba. Thirty years of teaching, coordinating, training, interpreting, and advising across two continents and multiple institutional contexts followed. A B.A. in Psychology and M.S. in Management from Lesley University, Cambridge MA. NAFSA Management Development Program, Washington DC. Cambridge C2 Proficiency. Ongoing study in financial regulation and compliance.

Ki Society membership and Ki Aikido practice inform everything — the quality of attention, the capacity to redirect rather than oppose, the commitment to presence over performance.

Kiprisma is the current form of a practice that has always been in motion.

Christopher Michael is an independent educator, consultant, and practitioner. His work draws on extensive training in psychology, management, and human development, rooted in these foundational disciplines across all aspects of his practice. Advisory and consulting services are complementary and non-transactional in scope. Reiki and energy sessions are offered as complementary wellness practices in support of emotional regulation and the alignment of mind, body, and expression.
